# Privacy Policy: Keep For Later

**Last Updated: July 22, 2026**

Your privacy is critically important to us. **Keep For Later** is designed to be a private, offline-first tool for saving your own content.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
*   **No Personal Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal information, such as your name, email address, location, or contacts.
*   **No Tracking**: The app does not contain any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your behavior or usage patterns.
*   **Local Storage**: All data you save (URLs, text snippets, and notes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you manually export it.

## 2. App Group Data Sharing
The app uses an **App Group** (`group.gy.roach.keepforlater`) to allow the Share Extension to send content to the main application. This communication happens entirely on-device and is managed by the iOS operating system.

## 3. Required Privacy Disclosures (iOS Privacy Manifest)
In accordance with Apple's requirements, we disclose the use of the following system APIs for non-tracking purposes:
*   **UserDefaults**: Used solely to store your app preferences (such as theme settings or sort order).
*   **File Timestamp**: Used by the internal database to ensure data consistency and synchronization between the app and its extension.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The app does not communicate with any third-party services. When you save a URL, the app may display the host name or title provided by the system share sheet, but it does not perform external "scraping" or data enrichment that would expose your IP address to third-party servers.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
